# Built-in Components

Three components ship with go-pretty-pdf out of the box — no
registration required, they just work.

<DeepDive title="DeepDive">
Use this for supplementary detail a reader can skip without losing the
thread of the main text: background, rationale, or an aside.
</DeepDive>

<DeepDive>
DeepDive also works without a title, for a lighter-weight aside.
</DeepDive>

<Warning title="Warning">
Use this to flag something a reader must not miss: a prerequisite, a
gotcha, or a breaking change.
</Warning>

<Axiom>
Axiom is for a single, quotable statement worth remembering on its own.
</Axiom>

<Axiom title="Named Axiom">
Axiom also accepts a title when the statement needs attribution.
</Axiom>
