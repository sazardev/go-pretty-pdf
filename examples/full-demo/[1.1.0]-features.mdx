## Variable Substitution

Define variables in `go-pretty-pdf.yml` under `vars:`:

```yaml
vars:
  product: "{{product}}"
  version: "{{version}}"
  company: "{{company}}"
```

Use them anywhere in your MDX: `{{"{{product}}"}}`, `{{"{{version}}"}}`.
